Next Schedules

The skill acquisition program is to go on for two weeks which is from the june 23, 2025 to the july 4, 2025. During this two weeks of the program, we have set out a schedule on how the program will run. This are important events that will occur during the days of the program.

Opening ceremony/Orientation

Welcome to Day One of our Skill Acquisition Program! Today, you'll get an overview of the course, its structure, and the skills you'll learn in web development and content creation. We'll also introduce the resources available to support your learning. We're excited to start this journey with you!.

Obaro Stanley

CEO / Founder

9:00 - 9:45 AM ICTC Center, UNIPORT

Introduction to Content Creation and Photography/Video Editing

On Day one of our program, you'll be introduced to the fundamentals of content creation, including basic photography and video editing techniques. This session will help you understand how to capture and craft visually engaging content, laying the groundwork for your journey in digital media. It's the first step toward building your skills in creating compelling content.

Derby Stone

Content Creator

10:00 - 11:45 AM ICTC Center, UNIPORT

Introduction to web development

On Day one, you'll learn the basics of building websites, including HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. The session covers fundamental coding concepts and tools used for web creation. By the end, you'll understand how websites are structured and be ready to start building your own.

Edison Ovicial

Web developer

12:00 - 2:00 PM ICTC Center, UNIPORT

Content creation and Photography/video editing

Day two focuses on capturing high-quality photos and videos, along with essential editing skills. You’ll learn basic techniques and tools to enhance your visuals, helping you create engaging content for various platforms.

Derby Stone

Content Creator

10:00 -11:45 AM Cyber security lab, UNIPORT

Web development

On day 2, participants learn the basics of HTML and CSS to build and style simple web pages. The focus is on creating structured, visually appealing websites and understanding responsive design. By the end of the day, you'll be able to start building your own web projects.

Edison ovicial

Web developer

12:00 - 02:00 PM ICTC Center, UNIPORT

Content Creation and Photography/Video Editing

On day nine, you'll learn how to create engaging content and edit photos and videos like a pro. By the end, you'll have the skills to share your story and stand out online.

Derby Stone

Content Creator

10:00 - 11:45 AM Cyber security lab, UNIPORT

Web development

On day nine you'll learn how to build your own websites, creating responsive and user-friendly pages. This skill will help you showcase your work and open new opportunities in the digital world before graduation.

Edison ovicial

Web developer

12:00 - 02:45 PM ICTC center, UNIPORT

Graduation Ceremony (Certificate Presentation)

At the end of the program, participants will celebrate their achievements during the graduation ceremony. Certificates will be awarded to recognize your dedication and newly acquired skills, marking your successful completion and readiness to pursue your passion in web development, content creation, or media editing.
